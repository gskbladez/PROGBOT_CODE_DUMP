**ProgBot is open-source and open to community suggestions!** If you'd like to help out, you can contribute to the GitHub repo at: <https://github.com/gskbladez/PROGBOT_CODE_DUMP>. Just create a fork from master and submit pull requests once you're ready!
Even if you can't contribute to the code, you can still help us by suggesting features or reporting issues! Use `bugreport` to send us a message, or contact the ProgBot development team directly!
